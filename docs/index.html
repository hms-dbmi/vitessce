Vitessce

This is a demo of key concepts for a visual integration tool for exploration of spatial single-cell experiments. This demo focusses on scalable, linked visualizations that support both spatial and non-spatial representation of cell-level and molecule-level data.

Vitessce is supported by the NIH Common Fund, through the Human BioMolecular Atlas Program (HuBMAP), Integration, Visualization & Engagement (HIVE) Initiative, RFA-RM-18-001.
